ABOUT

Welcome to Twin Trails, your local guide to discovering the best hiking, walking, and nature escapes across the Twin Cities and beyond. From the rugged bluffs overlooking the Mississippi River to the serene forest paths of Crosby Farm and the rolling hills of Lebanon Hills, our mission is to connect outdoor enthusiasts with the beauty tucked between Minneapolis and St. Paul’s urban edges. Whether you’re a weekend wanderer, trail runner, or curious first-timer, we’re here to help you explore the landscapes that make the Twin Cities truly unique. At Twin Trails, we believe adventure doesn’t have to be far from home. Our curated trail guides, seasonal updates, and insider tips highlight hidden gems, accessible routes, and community events that celebrate Minnesota’s vibrant outdoor culture. Lace up your boots, grab your water bottle, and come see how much wilderness waits just beyond the skyline.
